Focaccia with caramelized Onions,Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appertizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Fresh Cranberries Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingrediends

For the focaccia
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil olive, plus more for the pan
For the topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly scliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil,for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the doough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
